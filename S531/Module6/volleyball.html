Volleyball

I got acquainted with volleyball at the age of 30 when I started working at the school. I met colleagues from other schools who invited me to play traditional volleyball. We used to meet once a week on Fridays in the school gym. I wasn't doing well at first, but I am persistent.

I started taking lessons from a coach, and after about six months, things started to improve for me.

After two years, I stopped playing traditional volleyball and switched to beach volleyball. I like this type of volleyball more!

In beach volleyball, there are only 2 players in a team, whereas in traditional volleyball, there are 6 players in a team. That's why beach volleyball involves more activity and movement.

In the summer, we play outdoors, and in the winter, we play indoors.
