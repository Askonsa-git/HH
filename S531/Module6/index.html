Briefly about me

I was born in 1985 in a beautiful and picturesque place - the village of Bezrukovo, somewhere far in the south of Siberia.

In school, I studied well and read a lot. However, it wasn't always like that. At the beginning of my education, I preferred to spend time outdoors, and I hurried through my lessons, bringing home poor grades. Then my dad applied a disciplinary approach, stating that I could only go out if my grades were high.So, I made an effort to do my homework well, which took a lot of time.

All my friends, after a few unsuccessful attempts to invite me outside, stopped coming to see me. Over time, I became engrossed in my studies and became one of the best students. Reading books captivated me into the world of imagination.

  • At the age of 16, I started living independently: I moved to the city, studied at a pedagogical college, and worked simultaneously as a nanny.
  • At 20, I moved to the huge city of Moscow. In my childhood, I couldn't even dream that I would ever visit it.
  • At 26, I moved to St. Petersburg.
  • At 37, I moved to Finland.
