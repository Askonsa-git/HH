Seven-colors flower

Valentin Katayev

Note:This is a poem from a children's fairy tale about a magical flower that could make any wish come true. Seven - petals, seven desires. 6 wishes were useless, the girl made wishes for pleasure: toys, sweets, but there was no pleasure left after. And on the last petal she suddenly notices a boy with crutches...

Somewhere there is one flower,
It has seven petals,
I wish I could make a wish,
All your wishes!
Just tell me,
Where can I find that flower,
Where, where is he like this
My little seven-colors flower!

Fly, fly petal,
Through west to east,
Through the north, through the south,
Come back after making a circle!
Fly, fly petal,
Through west to east,
Fly around the Earth,
To be, in my opinion, led!
