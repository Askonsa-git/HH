About Us

Information about our company

On May 20, 2003, humanity witnessed a historical event - the first flight of the reusable spacecraft SpaceShipOne SpaceShipOne, which foreshadowed the era of private space tourism. The flight lasted 90 minutes.

SpaceShipOne rose to an altitude of more than 100 kilometers, reaching the edge of space known as the Pocket of Pocket. During the flight, the ship experienced weightlessness, and the pilot was able to enjoy breathtaking views of the Earth from space.

It was the beginning of space tourism.

Our company was founded in 2017. Since then, more than a thousand people have realized their dream - to see our planet from space. These emotions cannot be compared with anything.

The company's main office is located in California. Our company employs the most trusted and professional specialists.
